How to Play

  1. Press Space or Arrow Up (or tap) to flap upward.
  2. Each tap applies an upward flap — time your flaps to pass between the gaps.
  3. Avoid obstacles; the score increases the longer you survive.

Tips

  • Short, well-timed taps are more reliable than holding the key.
  • Watch obstacle spacing and recover quickly after a flap.
  • Play in a quiet environment to focus on rhythm.
